Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering (MPSTME) is a Private Institute located in Mumbai, Maharashtra and was established in the year 2006. The Institute is affiliated to NMIMS Deemed to be University, Mumbai and approved by University Grants Commission (UGC).

MPSTME is also approved by the National Council of Teacher Education (NCTE) and All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). The institute garnered Educational Leadership Award by ET Business Leader of the Year 2020. MPSTME students have the chance to study a part of their program at NMIMS and then go and complete rest of the course at a partner university abroad
